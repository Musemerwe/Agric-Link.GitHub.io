Welcome to Agric Link

The app operates with distinct workflows for farmers and suppliers, ensuring a smooth user experience tailored to their specific needs.

Farmer's Workflow:

Account Creation:

  • Register: Farmers will create an account by providing their email and password on the registration page.
  • Login: Farmers log in using their email and password on the login page.

Posting Products:

  • Farmer Account Page: After logging in, farmers navigate to their account page where they can upload images of their agricultural products and provide detailed information.
  • Uploading Images: Farmers use the image picker to select images from their device. The selected image is uploaded to Firebase Storage, and the URL is saved in Firestore.
  • Posting Details: Farmers fill out a form with product details such as name, category, quantity, price, location, and contact information. This information, along with the image URL, is stored in Firestore under a 'products' collection.

Supplier's Workflow:

Account Creation:

  • Register: Suppliers will create an account by providing their email and password on the registration page.
  • Login: Suppliers log in using their email and password on the login page.

Browsing Products:

  • Home Page: After logging in, suppliers land on the home page (`/home`), which displays a list of product categories (e.g., Fruits, Vegetables).
  • Selecting a Category: Suppliers click on a category to view a list of products within that category.

Viewing Product Details:

  • Product List Page: Upon selecting a category, suppliers are taken to the product list page (`/product_list`) where they can see all products in that category..
  • Selecting a Product: Suppliers click on a specific product to view more details.
  • Description of point 3.

Viewing Farmer Details:

  • Product Details Page: Suppliers are taken to a page (`/product_details`) displaying detailed information about the selected product, including the farmers who posted it, their quantity, price, location, and contact information.
  • Initiating Chat:Suppliers can initiate a chat with the farmer by clicking the 'Chat with Farmer' button.

Chat Functionality:
Chat Page: When a supplier clicks to chat with a farmer, they are taken to a chat page (`/chat`) where they can send and receive messages in real-time.
Real-time Messaging: Messages are stored in Firestore under a specific chat document for each farmer-supplier pair, and updates are reflected in real-time.
