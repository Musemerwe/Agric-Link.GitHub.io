The app works for both farmers and suppliers:

For Farmers:

Account Setup:
Farmers sign up and log in using their credentials.
Posting Products:
Farmers upload product photos and details like name, category, etc.

For Suppliers:

Account Setup:
Suppliers create accounts and log in.
Browsing and Selecting Products:
Suppliers browse categories, view product details, and contact farmers.
Communication:
Suppliers can chat with farmers in real-time.
