Agric Link App Workflow

Overview

The Agric Link app operates with distinct workflows tailored for farmers and suppliers, ensuring a smooth user experience and efficient transactions in the agricultural sector.

Farmer's Workflow

  1. Account Creation

    • Register: Farmers create an account by providing their email and password on the registration page (/register).
    • Login: Farmers log in using their credentials on the login page (/login).

  2. Posting Products

    • Farmer Account Page: After logging in, farmers navigate to their account page (/farmer_account), where they can upload images of their agricultural products.
    • Uploading Images: Farmers use the image picker to select images from their device, which are uploaded to Firebase Storage.
    • Posting Details: Farmers fill out a form with product details such as name, category, quantity, price, location, and contact information. This information is stored in Firestore under a 'products' collection.

Supplier's Workflow

  1. Account Creation

    • Register: Suppliers create an account by providing their email and password on the registration page (/register).
    • Login: Suppliers log in using their credentials on the login page (/login).

  2. Browsing Products

    • Home Page: After logging in, suppliers land on the home page (/home), displaying a list of product categories (e.g., Fruits, Vegetables).
    • Selecting a Category: Suppliers click on a category to view a list of products within that category.

  3. Viewing Product Details

    • Product List Page: Upon selecting a category, suppliers are taken to the product list page (/product_list), where they can see all products in that category.
    • Selecting a Product: Suppliers click on a specific product to view more details, including the farmers who posted it, quantity, price, location, and contact information.

  4. Initiating Chat

    • Product Details Page: Suppliers can initiate a chat with the farmer by clicking the 'Chat with Farmer' button on the product details page (/product_details).

Chat Functionality

  • Chat Page: When a supplier initiates a chat with a farmer, they are taken to a chat page (/chat) where they can send and receive messages in real-time.
  • Real-time Messaging: Messages are stored in Firestore under a specific chat document for each farmer-supplier pair, ensuring real-time updates.

Technical Details

  1. Firebase Authentication: Manages user registration and login securely.
  2. Firestore Database: Stores user information, product details, and chat messages.
  3. Firebase Storage: Stores images uploaded by farmers and provides URLs for integration.
  4. Image Picker: Allows farmers to select and upload images of products.
  5. Provider: Manages state within the app for data consistency and smooth user experience.
  6. Google Maps Integration: Utilizes Google Maps API in product_map_page.dart to display farmer locations.

Example Scenarios

Scenario 1: Farmer Posts a Product

  1. Farmer registers/logs in and navigates to the Farmer Account Page.
  2. Farmer uploads an image of a product (e.g., apples) and enters details such as category, quantity, price, location, and contact information.
  3. Product details and image URL are saved in Firestore under the 'products' collection.

Scenario 2: Supplier Browses and Chats

  1. Supplier registers/logs in and navigates to the Home Page.
  2. Supplier selects a category (e.g., Fruits) and views a list of products within that category.
  3. Supplier clicks on a specific product to view details, including the farmer's contact information.
  4. Supplier initiates a chat with the farmer to discuss the product further.
